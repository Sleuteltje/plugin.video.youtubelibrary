=============== CUT UP ALBUM VIDEOS BY SONGS ================
If the Description contains a tracklist:

00:23: Artist - Song
04:12: Artist2 - Song

We could cut up the album back to its original songs. In the above example, we can guess that 00:23-04:11 is song1, and 04:12 - end of video  is song2.

Then if the song1 is being played, we can jump the player to 00:23. When the player reaches 04:11, it can automatically stop the player / move the player to the next song.

Tracknumbers could be identified as the order of the tracklist. And the title could be used as Artist - Album.
